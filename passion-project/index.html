Who are the Muppets????

The Muppets are a group of frogs and dogs and bears and chickens and whatever recognized for their unique blend of absurdist humor, slapstick comedy, and self-aware musical variety sketches. The Muppets have been in many films, television shows, music, and other related media spaning decades. This site is dedicated to introucing you to a few of the many members.
