Meet Kermit

Kermit the Frog is the heart and soul of the Muppets. He is best known as the calm, witty, and often exasperated host of The Muppet Show and a key figure in Sesame Street and various Muppet films and specials. He is a a thoughtful, slightly frazzled dreamer who leads with kindness, keeps his cool (most of the time), and always believes in the power of friendship, creativity, and a better tomorrow.
