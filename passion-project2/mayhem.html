Meet The Electric Mayhem

The Electric Mayhem, fka Dr Teeth and The Electric Mayhem, is the
Muppets' resident rock band a colorful, chaotic, and endlessly groovy
ensemble that brings wild energy, psychedelic vibes, and serious musical
talent to the Muppet crew. There is Dr. Teeth the bands leader and
keyboardist (bottom middle), Animal on drums (top right), Floyd Pepper
on bass and vocals (top left), Janice on guitar and vocals (bottom left),
Zoot on saxophone (bottom right), and Lips on trumpet (top middle). The
Electric Mayhem's sound is positively funky blending classic rock, funk,
jazz, and pure mayhem.
