Detour
You have come across a Detour. You have to choose one of two tasks to complete to receive your next clue.
Choice A: Columns
Teams must travel to Binbirdirek Cistern and find four columns on a grid using a provided map with coordinates. On the columns were numbers, which they must use to unlock a padlocked box which contained their next clue.
Choice B: Kilos
Teams must travel to the Yeni Mosque, located at the old Jewish quarter of Eminönü, and find a man with a box of scales. Using a scale, teams would have to weigh enough people on the street until they reached a total of 2,500 kilograms (5,500 lbs). Once reached, teams must return their scale and show their weights to the man who would give them their next clue.
